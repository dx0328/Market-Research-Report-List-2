<p><strong>The Magnetic Coupling Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impedance Ratio:1：1</li><li>Impedance Ratio：4：1</li><li>Impedance Ratio:8：1</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic coupling transformers are categorized based on their impedance ratios, with the most common types being 1:1, 4:1, and 8:1. A 1:1 impedance ratio means that the input and output impedances are equal, providing balanced voltage signals. A 4:1 impedance ratio indicates a step-down transformer that reduces voltage levels, while an 8:1 ratio signifies a larger reduction in voltage. Other impedance ratios are also available in the market to suit specific applications and requirements, offering a wide range of options for various electrical systems.</p></p>
