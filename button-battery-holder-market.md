<p><p>Button battery holders are available in various types to suit different applications. Surface mount holders are soldered directly onto the surface of a PCB, making them compact and suitable for small devices. Through hole holders have pins that are inserted through holes on the PCB and soldered on the other side, providing strong mechanical connection. Other types of holders include snap-in or screw-mount holders, offering flexibility in installation. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
