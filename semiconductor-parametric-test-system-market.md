<p><p>A Semiconductor Parametric Test System is used for testing and measuring the electrical characteristics of semiconductor devices. Static parameter testing focuses on measuring and analyzing the steady-state characteristics of the devices, such as voltage, current, and resistance. Dynamic parameter testing, on the other hand, focuses on analyzing the performance of the devices under changing conditions, such as frequency or temperature variations. Both types of testing are crucial for ensuring the reliability and functionality of semiconductor devices in various applications.</p></p>
