<p><ul><li>OpenLoopScrubbers</li><li>ClosedLoopScrubbers</li><li>HybridScrubbers</li><li>MembraneScrubbers</li><li>DryScrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine scrubbers are devices used to reduce emissions from ship engines. There are several types of scrubbers available in the market. Open-loop scrubbers use sea water to clean exhaust gases, while closed-loop scrubbers use a circulating solution. Hybrid scrubbers combine both open and closed-loop technologies. Membrane scrubbers rely on chemical reactions to clean emissions, and dry scrubbers use a dry sorbent. Each type of scrubber has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs of ship operators.</p></p>
