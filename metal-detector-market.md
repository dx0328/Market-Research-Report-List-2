<p><strong>The Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal detector market consists of three main types - Pulse Induction, Very Low Frequency, and Beat-frequency Oscillation. Pulse Induction detectors are great for deep searching and can ignore mineralization in the ground. Very Low Frequency detectors are ideal for discriminating between different types of metals and have good sensitivity to small objects. Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors provide good depth penetration and are suitable for use in highly mineralized soil. Each type caters to specific needs in the metal detection industry.</p></p>
