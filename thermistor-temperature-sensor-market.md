<p><p>The Thermistor Temperature Sensor market can be segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact thermistor temperature sensors require physical contact with the object being measured, providing precise and accurate temperature readings. Non-contact thermistor temperature sensors, on the other hand, use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance without touching the object. These sensors are ideal for measuring temperature in hard-to-reach or hazardous locations. Ultimately, both types of thermistor temperature sensors offer specific benefits depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
