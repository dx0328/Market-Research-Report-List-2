<p><p>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) technology consists of two main types: PMOS (Positive Metal Oxide Semiconductor) and NMOS (Negative Metal Oxide Semiconductor). PMOS is designed to operate with positive voltage, while NMOS is designed to operate with negative voltage. The combination of PMOS and NMOS in CMOS technology allows for low power consumption, high speed, and high noise immunity. Both PMOS and NMOS have their unique characteristics and applications, making them suitable for various electronic devices and circuits.</p></p>
