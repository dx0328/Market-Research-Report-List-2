<p><p>Tumor embolization market includes various types such as Trans-Arterial Embolization (TAE), Drug-Eluting Bead Chemoembolization (DEB-TACE), Radioembolization (RE), and others. TAE involves blocking blood flow to the tumor by injecting substances into the artery supplying it. DEB-TACE delivers chemotherapy drugs directly to the tumor using tiny beads. Radioembolization uses radiation-emitting microspheres to target and treat tumors. Other methods may involve different embolic agents or delivery mechanisms for treating tumors through embolization procedures.</p></p>
