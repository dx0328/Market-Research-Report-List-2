<p><p>Electro Photographic Printing technology is used in two main market types - Monochrome Printing and Color Printing. Monochrome Printing produces black and white prints, ideal for documents and simple graphics. Color Printing offers the ability to produce colorful, vibrant prints suitable for marketing materials, brochures, and photographs. Both types of printing rely on the same electro photographic process, with the main difference being the use of only black toner for monochrome printing and the addition of colored toners for color printing.</p></p>
