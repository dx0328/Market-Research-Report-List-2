The two main types of converters are two-way and three-way converters. Two-way converters primarily reduce emissions of carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, while three-way converters also reduce nitrogen oxides. Other markets in the automotive catalytic converter industry may include high-performance converters, diesel converters, and electric vehicle converters, each designed to meet specific emission control needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
