<p><p>Water clarifiers market can be categorized into three types based on their design and shape - rectangular, circular, and others. Rectangular water clarifiers are typically used for large scale water treatment plants and are known for their efficiency in separating solid particles from water. Circular clarifiers are commonly used in wastewater treatment facilities due to their compact design and ease of maintenance. Other types of water clarifiers include inclined plate settlers, tube settlers, and dissolved air flotation clarifiers, each serving specific purposes in water treatment processes.</p></p>
