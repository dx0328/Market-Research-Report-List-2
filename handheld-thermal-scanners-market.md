<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld thermal scanners are divided into different market types based on the wavelength range they operate in. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) scanners are ideal for applications requiring high resolution and sensitivity in various lighting conditions. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) scanners are commonly used for industrial and defense applications due to their ability to detect temperature variations accurately. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) scanners are popular in medical, industrial, and surveillance sectors for their ability to detect heat signatures from objects or individuals at long distances.</p></p>
