<p><p>The defibrillator market includes various types of devices designed to monitor and treat cardiac arrhythmias. Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) are portable devices used by non-medical personnel. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor and treat abnormal heart rhythms. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators provide temporary protection for those at risk for sudden cardiac arrest. The Advanced Life Support (ALS) market includes defibrillators used by healthcare professionals to provide immediate, high-level care in emergency situations.</p></p>
