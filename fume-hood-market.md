<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fume hood market is segmented into two main types: ductless fume hoods and ducted fume hoods. </p><p>Ductless fume hoods are self-contained units that filter and recirculate air within the hood, making them suitable for spaces where ductwork is not feasible. They are cost-effective and environmentally friendly.</p><p>On the other hand, ducted fume hoods are connected to an external ventilation system that removes contaminated air from the laboratory. They are ideal for applications requiring the removal of toxic or volatile chemicals.</p></p>
