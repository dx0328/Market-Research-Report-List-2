<p><p>Rubber floor coverings can be classified into two main types: homogeneity and heterogeneity. Homogeneous rubber floor coverings are made of a single type of material throughout, providing a uniform look and feel. On the other hand, heterogeneous rubber floor coverings consist of multiple layers of different materials, creating a more diverse and customizable aesthetic. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in terms of design, durability, and maintenance, offering a wide range of options for consumers in the market.</p></p>
