<p><strong>The Capacitive Touchscreen Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Capacitive Pen</li><li>Passive Capacitive Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive touchscreen pens come in two main types: active and passive. Active capacitive pens use a built-in power source, usually a battery, to transmit signals to the touchscreen device, allowing for more precise and responsive touch input. Passive capacitive pens, on the other hand, do not require a power source and rely on the electrical charge from the touchscreen device to work. Active pens tend to be more expensive and offer advanced features, while passive pens are simpler and more affordable.</p></p>
