<p><ul><li>Manual Wire Bonders</li><li>Semi-Automatic Wire Bonders</li><li>Fully-Automatic Wire Bonders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire bonders are machines used in the semiconductor industry to connect integrated circuits to semiconductor packages. There are three main types of wire bonders in the market - manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manual wire bonders require operators to manually place and bond wires, while semi-automatic bonders assist in wire alignment but still require human intervention. Fully-automatic wire bonders are capable of fully automated wire bonding processes without the need for operator intervention, increasing efficiency and productivity in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
