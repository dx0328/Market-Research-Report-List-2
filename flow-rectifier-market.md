<p><p>Flow rectifiers are devices used to convert alternating current into direct current and are categorized into two main types based on power capacity: high power rectifiers and low power rectifiers. High power rectifiers are designed for industrial applications requiring high voltage and current, such as in welding machines and power supplies for industrial machinery. On the other hand, low power rectifiers are commonly used in consumer electronics and small-scale industrial equipment that require lower voltage and current levels, such as battery chargers and LED drivers.</p></p>
