<p><p>A chilled beam system is a type of HVAC system that uses convection and radiation to cool or heat a space. <p><p>Chilled beam systems are categorized into three market types based on their installation method: concealed, exposed, and recessed. Concealed chilled beam systems are hidden from view within the ceiling, providing a streamlined and aesthetically pleasing appearance. Exposed chilled beam systems are visible within the room and are often chosen for their industrial or modern design appeal. Recessed chilled beam systems are partially integrated into the ceiling, offering a compromise between visibility and aesthetics. Each market type caters to different preferences and architectural requirements.</p></p>
