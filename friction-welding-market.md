<p><p>Friction welding is a versatile process with various market types, including Linear Friction Welding, Rotary Friction Welding, and Stir Friction Welding. Linear Friction Welding involves rubbing two materials together in a linear motion to create a strong bond. Rotary Friction Welding involves rotating one material against another to create a weld. Stir Friction Welding involves mixing two materials together through frictional heat and pressure. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
