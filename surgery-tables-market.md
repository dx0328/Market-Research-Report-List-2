<p><p>Surgery tables come in two main types: motorized and non-motorized. Motorized tables are equipped with electronic controls that allow for easy adjustment of height, tilt, and positioning during surgical procedures. These tables are ideal for complex surgeries that require precise movements and positioning. On the other hand, non-motorized tables are manually adjusted and are often used for simpler procedures or in settings where electronic controls are not necessary. Both types of tables play a crucial role in providing a stable and comfortable platform for surgical procedures.</p></p>
