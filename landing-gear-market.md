<p><p>Strut Landing Gear, Rocker Landing Gear, Framed Landing Gear, and Pontoon Landing Gear are four main types of landing gear used in different aircrafts. Strut Landing Gear consists of one or more hydraulic struts for shock absorption. Rocker Landing Gear uses a pivoting mechanism for shock absorption. Framed Landing Gear has a rigid frame for stability during touchdown. Pontoon Landing Gear uses floats attached to the aircraft for landing on water surfaces. Each type has its own unique design and function to ensure safe and efficient landings for different aircraft.</p></p>
