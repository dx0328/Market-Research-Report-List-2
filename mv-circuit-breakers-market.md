<p><ul><li>Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB)</li><li>Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB)</li><li>Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MV Circuit Breakers market consists of three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are designed for low voltage applications and provide protection against overcurrents. MCCBs are used for higher voltage systems and offer protection against short circuits and overloads. ACBs are typically used in large commercial and industrial installations to provide reliable protection and control over electrical circuits. Each type of circuit breaker serves a specific purpose in ensuring electrical safety and functionality.</p></p>
