<p><p>General Cold Cathode Gauges are widely used in industries for measuring low to high vacuum levels. They are cost-effective and offer reliable performance. On the other hand, Corrosion Resistant Cold Cathode Gauges are specifically designed for environments with high levels of corrosive gases, such as in semiconductor manufacturing or chemical processing industries. These gauges are constructed from materials that can withstand corrosive conditions, ensuring accurate and long-lasting vacuum measurements. Both types of gauges play a crucial role in maintaining optimal vacuum levels in various industrial processes.</p></p>
