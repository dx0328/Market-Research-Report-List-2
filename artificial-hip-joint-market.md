<p><p>Artificial hip joint market types refer to different combinations of materials used in hip replacement surgeries. Metal-on-Metal implants consist of metal components that rub against each other. Metal-on-Polyethylene implants have a metal ball that moves inside a plastic socket. Ceramic-on-Metal implants have a ceramic ball and a metal socket. Ceramic-on-Polyethylene implants use a ceramic ball and a plastic socket. Ceramic-on-Ceramic implants have both components made of ceramic. Each type has its own advantages and considerations for patients undergoing hip replacement surgery.</p></p>
