<p><p>Transcutaneous Pacing Market includes three main types: Single Chamber Temporary Pacemaker, Dual Chamber Temporary Pacemaker, and Triple Chamber Temporary Pacemaker. Single chamber pacemakers deliver electrical impulses to one chamber of the heart, dual chamber pacemakers deliver impulses to two chambers, and triple chamber pacemakers deliver impulses to three chambers. These devices are used for temporary pacing in patients with abnormal heart rhythms or conduction disorders. Each type offers different levels of control and effectiveness in managing heart conditions, providing healthcare professionals with options to meet individual patient needs.</p></p>
