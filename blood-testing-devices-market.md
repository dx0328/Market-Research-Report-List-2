<p><p>Blood testing devices are classified into two main types: bench-top and portable. Bench-top devices are larger, stationary machines typically found in laboratories and hospitals. They offer high throughput and accuracy, making them suitable for conducting a large number of tests. On the other hand, portable devices are smaller, more portable, and easy to carry around. They are ideal for point-of-care testing, allowing healthcare providers to perform tests in remote or non-traditional settings. Both types of devices play a critical role in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions.</p></p>
