<p><p>High Voltage Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) is classified into different market types based on their design and functionality. Isolated Phase GIS utilizes separate enclosures for each phase to ensure safety and reliability. Integrated 3 Phase GIS integrates all three phases into a single compact unit, reducing footprint and installation time. Hybrid GIS combines both air-insulated and gas-insulated components for improved performance. Other market types may include specialty GIS designed for specific applications or industries. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
