<p><p>IC cards and smart cards are used for various applications, with two main market types - contact and contactless. Contact cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transmission. On the other hand, contactless cards use radio-frequency technology to communicate with the card reader wirelessly. Contact cards are commonly used in access control systems and payment terminals, while contactless cards are more prevalent in transportation systems and mobile payments. Both market types play a crucial role in enhancing security and convenience in different industries.</p></p>
