<p><p>Electronic prototyping market consists of two main types: PCB prototyping and electronic product prototyping. PCB prototyping involves creating a prototype circuit board to test the functionality and design of electronic devices. This market segment caters to engineers and designers looking to validate their designs before mass production. Electronic product prototyping, on the other hand, involves creating a physical prototype of the final electronic product to test its functionality and user experience. Both types of prototyping play a crucial role in the product development process.</p></p>
