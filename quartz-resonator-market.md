<p><p>Quartz resonators are commonly available in two main market types: Dip (Dual In-line Package) and SMD (Surface Mount Device). Dip resonators are inserted into circuit boards using through-hole technology, making them easier to replace and repair. On the other hand, SMD resonators are designed for surface mounting directly onto circuit boards, saving space and allowing for automated assembly processes. Both types of resonators offer precise frequency control and stability, making them essential components in a wide range of electronic devices.</p></p>
