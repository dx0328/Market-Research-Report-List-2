<p><p>The Automotive Air Quality Sensor market includes double sensor and triple sensor types. Double sensor systems incorporate two sensors to measure different air quality parameters such as volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and carbon dioxide levels. Triple sensor systems add a third sensor to measure additional air quality factors such as particulate matter. Both types of sensors are used in automotive applications to monitor and improve overall air quality inside vehicles, ensuring a safer and healthier driving environment for passengers.</p></p>
