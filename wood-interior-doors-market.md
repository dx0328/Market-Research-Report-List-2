<p><p>Wood interior doors come in two main market types: hardwood and softwood. Hardwood interior doors are typically made from wood sourced from deciduous trees like oak, mahogany, or cherry. These doors are known for their durability, strength, and high-end appearance. Softwood interior doors, on the other hand, are made from wood sourced from coniferous trees like pine, cedar, or spruce. These doors are typically less expensive than hardwood doors and may require more maintenance over time. Both types of wood interior doors offer a range of styles, finishes, and customization options to suit any interior design aesthetic.</p></p>
