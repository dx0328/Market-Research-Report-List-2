<p><strong>The Electric Wheelchair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li><li>Wet-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric wheelchairs are classified into two main types based on the battery they use: dry-battery electric wheelchairs and wet-battery electric wheelchairs. Dry-battery electric wheelchairs are powered by maintenance-free batteries that do not require regular refilling of distilled water. On the other hand, wet-battery electric wheelchairs are powered by lead-acid batteries that need regular maintenance and refilling of distilled water. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between them depends on the user's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
