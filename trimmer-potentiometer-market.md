<p><p>Trimmer potentiometers are available in two main types: single turn and multi turn. Single turn trimmers allow for adjustment over one complete rotation, making them ideal for applications where finer adjustments are required. Multi turn trimmers, on the other hand, provide precision adjustments over multiple rotations, making them suitable for applications where a high degree of accuracy is needed. Both types offer various options in terms of resistance values and packages, catering to a wide range of requirements in the market.</p></p>
