<p><strong>The Wind Energy Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doubly-fed Power Converter</li><li>Full Power Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wind energy converters can be categorized into two main types: Doubly-fed Power Converters and Full Power Converters. Doubly-fed Power Converters utilize a partially rated power converter with a portion of the power flow directly fed into the rotor, while Full Power Converters use a fully-rated power converter to control the entire power output. Both types offer different levels of control and efficiency, with Full Power Converters generally being more advanced and cost-effective in the market.</p></p>
