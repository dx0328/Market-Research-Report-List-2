<p><p>PV inverters are essential components in solar power systems, converting DC electricity generated by solar panels into AC electricity for use in homes and businesses. Three main types of PV inverters are String Inverters, Central Inverters, and Microinverters. String Inverters are cost-effective and efficient for small to medium-sized systems. Central Inverters are suitable for large-scale solar installations, offering high power capacity. Microinverters are installed on each solar panel, providing greater flexibility and efficiency, especially in systems with shading or varying panel orientations.</p></p>
