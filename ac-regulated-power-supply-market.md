<p><p>AC Regulated Power Supply market is classified into two main types, namely Non-Contact AC Regulated Power Supply and Contact AC Regulated Power Supply. Non-Contact AC Regulated Power Supply uses wireless technology to transfer power to devices without direct physical contact, making it suitable for applications where physical connections are not feasible.
