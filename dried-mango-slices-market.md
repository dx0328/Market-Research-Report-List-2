<p><p>Dried mango slices are available in various market types such as freeze-dried, vacuum drying, and others. Freeze-dried mango slices are produced by freezing the fruit and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in a crispy texture and strong mango flavor. Vacuum drying involves removing moisture from the fruit under low pressure, preserving the natural color and flavor. Other methods may include solar drying or air drying, each offering unique characteristics and textures to the dried mango slices.</p></p>
