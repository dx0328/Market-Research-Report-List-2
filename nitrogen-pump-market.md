<p><p>Nitrogen pumps are commonly used in industries like oil and gas, mining, and chemical processing. Two major types of nitrogen pumps in the market are triplex pumps and quintuplex pumps. Triplex pumps have three plungers that move back and forth to create the pumping action, while quintuplex pumps have five plungers. Triplex pumps are suitable for medium-duty applications, while quintuplex pumps are ideal for high-pressure and heavy-duty operations. Both types offer efficient and reliable nitrogen pumping capabilities for various industrial applications.</p></p>
