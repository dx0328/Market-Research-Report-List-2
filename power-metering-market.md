<p><p>Analog meters are traditional electric meters that measure power consumption with rotating dials. Digital meters use electronic circuits to provide accurate readings of electricity usage. Smart meters are advanced devices that not only monitor energy usage in real time but also enable two-way communication between the meter and the utility company. The power metering market is evolving with the increasing adoption of smart meters due to their efficiency and ability to enable a smart grid system.</p></p>
