<p><strong>The Radon Measurement Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Term Radon Test</li><li>Short Term Radon Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long-term radon tests typically last for at least 90 days and provide a more accurate measurement of average radon levels in a home. These tests are usually conducted using passive devices such as charcoal canisters or alpha track detectors. On the other hand, short-term radon tests can be completed within a few days and are often used for quickly assessing radon levels. These tests are commonly conducted using active devices like continuous radon monitors or charcoal liquid scintillation detectors.</p></p>
