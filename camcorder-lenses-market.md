<p><p>Camcorder lenses can be divided into two main types: Optical Zoom Lenses and Fixed Focus Lenses. Optical Zoom Lenses allow users to adjust the focal length of the lens to bring distant objects closer, providing greater flexibility in framing shots. Fixed Focus Lenses, on the other hand, have a set focal length and do not offer the ability to zoom in or out. Both types of lenses have their advantages and are suited for different shooting situations.</p></p>
