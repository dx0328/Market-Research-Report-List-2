<p><p>Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis are both treatments for chronic kidney disease that help to remove waste and fluid from the blood when the kidneys are no longer able to do so effectively. </p><p>Hemodialysis involves running blood through a filter outside the body to remove waste and excess fluid and then returning the clean blood back to the body. This process is typically done three times a week in a medical facility. Peritoneal Dialysis, on the other hand, involves using the lining of the abdomen (peritoneum) as a filter to remove waste and excess fluid. This can be done at home on a daily basis.</p><p>The Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic kidney disease, technological advancements in dialysis equipment, and a rising awareness about kidney health. Additionally, the growing geriatric population and the rising prevalence of conditions like diabetes and hypertension, which are risk factors for kidney disease, are expected to drive market growth in the coming years. </p><p>Overall, the Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis Market is projected to experience significant growth in the near future.</p></p>
