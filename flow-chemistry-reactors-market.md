CSTRs provide uniform mixing of reagents, PFRs enable efficient heat transfer and rapid reaction rates, MRTs offer high surface area-to-volume ratio and precise control over reaction conditions. Other types include fixed bed reactors, oscillatory flow reactors, and multistage reactors, each offering unique benefits for specific applications in the flow chemistry industry.</p></p>
