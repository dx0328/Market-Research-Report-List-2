<p><strong>The Inkjet Print Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Technology</li><li>Random Inkjet Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inkjet Print Machine Market can be categorized into two main types based on technology: Continuous Inkjet Technology and Random Inkjet Technology. Continuous Inkjet Technology involves a continuous stream of ink droplets being ejected onto the printing surface, allowing for high-speed and high-resolution printing. On the other hand, Random Inkjet Technology uses a random pattern of droplets to create images, providing flexibility and versatility in printing applications. Both technologies cater to different requirements in the printing industry, offering diverse options for businesses and consumers.</p></p>
