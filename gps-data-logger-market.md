They are typically used for recording and analyzing tracking information. Active GPS data loggers not only store data but also transmit it in real-time to a central server or monitoring system. This allows for instant tracking and monitoring of assets or vehicles. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as transportation, logistics, and field service management.</p></p>
