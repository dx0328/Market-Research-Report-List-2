<p><p>Remote Telemetry Units (RTUs) are devices used to monitor and control equipment remotely. There are two main types of RTU markets: Wireless RTU and Wired RTU. Wireless RTUs use cellular or radio technology to transmit data wirelessly, providing flexibility and ease of installation. On the other hand, Wired RTUs use physical connections such as Ethernet or serial cables to transmit data, offering reliable and secure communication. Both types of RTUs play a crucial role in various industries by ensuring real-time monitoring and control of assets.</p></p>
