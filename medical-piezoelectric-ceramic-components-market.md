<p><ul><li>PZT-Based</li><li>PMN-Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical piezoelectric ceramic components market consists of three main types - PZT-Based, PMN-Based, and Others. PZT-Based components are primarily composed of lead zirconate titanate and are widely used in medical devices for their high piezoelectric properties. PMN-Based components are made of lead magnesium niobate and are preferred for their exceptional electromechanical performance. The Others category encompasses various other ceramic materials used in medical applications. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs in the medical industry.</p></p>
