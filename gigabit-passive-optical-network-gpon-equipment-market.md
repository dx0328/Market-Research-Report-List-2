<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment market comprises three main types of equipment - Optical Line Terminal (OLT), Optical Network Terminal (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. The Optical Line Terminal (OLT) serves as the main point of aggregation, managing the data traffic between the service provider and the end-users. The Optical Network Terminal (ONT) is installed at the customer's premises to convert optical signals into data for use by end devices. Passive Optical Splitters are used to split and distribute the optical signal to multiple ONTs.</p></p>
