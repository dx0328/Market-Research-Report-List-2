<p><p>Apple Pectin is available in two main forms in the market: Dry Pectin and Liquid Pectin. Dry Pectin is a powdered form of pectin that is commonly used in making jams and jellies, as it helps to thicken the mixture. On the other hand, Liquid Pectin is a more convenient form of pectin that is often used for recipes that require a faster setting time. Both types of pectin offer similar benefits in terms of thickening and setting properties, but they differ in their application and usage.</p></p>
