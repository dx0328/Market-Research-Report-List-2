<p><p>The CMP Slurry Monitoring market consists of different types of monitoring practices such as Large Particle Counts (LPC), Density, Viscosity, and Others. Large Particle Counts (LPC) monitoring focuses on measuring the number of large particles present in the slurry, helping to prevent equipment damage. Density monitoring measures the concentration of solid particles in the slurry, ensuring optimal polishing performance. Viscosity monitoring assesses the thickness or flow behavior of the slurry, while Other monitoring practices may include pH or temperature monitoring for overall slurry quality control.</p></p>
