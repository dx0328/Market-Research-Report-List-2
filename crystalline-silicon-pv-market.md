<p><p>Crystalline Silicon PV market consists of two main types: Mono-Si Modules and Multi-Si Modules. Mono-Si Modules use solar cells made from single crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency but at a higher cost. Multi-Si Modules use solar cells made from multiple silicon crystals, providing a more cost-effective option with slightly lower efficiency. Both types are widely used in the solar industry, with Mono-Si Modules typically preferred for applications where space is limited and efficiency is crucial, while Multi-Si Modules are chosen for larger installations where cost is a primary concern.</p></p>
