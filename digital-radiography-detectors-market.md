<p><strong>The Digital Radiography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Direct Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Computed radiography (CR) Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital radiography detectors are essential components in modern medical imaging systems. Indirect flat panel detectors convert X-ray photons into light, which is then transformed into a digital signal. Direct flat panel detectors directly convert X-ray photons into electrical signals. Computed radiography (CR) detectors use a phosphor plate to capture X-ray images, which are then scanned and converted into digital images. Each type of detector offers unique advantages and is used in different medical imaging applications.</p></p>
