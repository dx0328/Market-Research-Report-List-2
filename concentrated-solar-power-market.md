<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough Collector</li><li>Solar Tower</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) market consists of three main types: Parabolic Trough Collector, Solar Tower, and Others. Parabolic Trough Collector systems use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver tube, producing heat to generate electricity. Solar Tower systems use a central receiver located at the top of a tower to collect and store solar energy. The "Others" category includes technologies such as dish systems and linear Fresnel reflectors. Each of these technologies has its own unique advantages and applications in the CSP market.</p></p>
