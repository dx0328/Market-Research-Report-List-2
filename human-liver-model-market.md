<p><p>The Human Liver Model market consists of various types such as Liver Organoids, Liver-on-a-chip, 2D Models, 3D Bioprinting, and others. Liver Organoids are miniaturized versions of the liver that mimic its structure and function. Liver-on-a-chip is a microfluidic device that replicates liver functions. 2D Models are flat liver cell cultures used for drug testing. 3D Bioprinting uses additive manufacturing to create three-dimensional liver models. Other types include spheroid cultures, co-culture systems, and liver slices for research and drug development purposes.</p></p>
