<p><p>Hardness testing machines are used to measure the resistance a material has to indentation or scratching. The main types of hardness testing machines in the market are Vickers, Rockwell, Brinell, Universal, and others. Vickers testing uses a diamond indenter, Rockwell uses different combinations of indenters and loads, while Brinell uses a hardened steel ball. Universal testing machines can accommodate various testing methods. Other types may include Knoop and Shore hardness testing. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
