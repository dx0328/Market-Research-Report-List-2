<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial digital cameras market can be categorized into two main types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption and fast readout speeds, making them ideal for applications requiring high frame rates. On the other hand, CCD cameras are preferred for their high sensitivity and low noise levels, making them suitable for low-light imaging. Both types of cameras have their own advantages and are used in various industrial applications depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
