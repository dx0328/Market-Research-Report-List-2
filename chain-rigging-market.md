<p><p>Chain rigging market can be categorized into two types: Single Chain Rigging and Multiple Chain Rigging. Single Chain Rigging involves using a single chain to lift and move heavy objects, commonly used in smaller scale applications. On the other hand, Multiple Chain Rigging utilizes multiple chains to distribute weight evenly and increase the lifting capacity, making it suitable for larger and more complex lifting operations. Both types play a crucial role in the rigging industry, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
